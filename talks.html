Difficult Conversations

I offer a half or full-day programme called "Difficult Conversations", which includes live poetry readings that provoke thought and discussion.

Who is this for?

This programme is mainly tailored for school students, aged 16 and over and buisinesses, who are experiencing issues with sexism, violence or discrimination.

What's it about?

I will discuss societal issues, such as racism, misogyny, violence and forms of discrimination that are present in our society.
