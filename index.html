Hi,
I am The Beardy Bloke!

I started writing poetry a few years ago after discovering that I am shockingly bad at journaling, talking to people about my feelings or writing a diary.

My real name is Mark Webb. I am a 40plus-year-old teacher in a huge inner city college who writes down his rambling thoughts in verse.

My book is not your normal poetry book though. In Volume One, I have included 25 poems and, at the back of the book, have written a brief description of why I wrote each one, where I was in my life and what I was thinking.

I hope that with this additional insight people will get the opportunity to appreciate and relate to the poems a whole lot more.
